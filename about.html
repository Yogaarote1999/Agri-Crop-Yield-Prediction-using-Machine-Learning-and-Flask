About Our Project

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the technology behind AgriDetect

Project Overview

AgriDetect is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to assist farmers with early disease detection and accurate crop yield prediction. Using deep learning, environmental data analysis, and modern image-processing technologies, our system provides actionable insights to improve crop health, boost productivity, and maximize farming profits.

As climate instability and crop diseases continue to threaten global agriculture, AgriDetect empowers farmers with real-time intelligence and automated analysis to support smart and sustainable farming.
